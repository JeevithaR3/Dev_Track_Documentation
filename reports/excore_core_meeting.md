## Overview

## Session Summary  

The meeting began with a round of introductions, followed by an open discussion where each ex-core member shared their journey and experiences during their tenure at DevTrack.  

They elaborated on the **core principles** that contributed to the club’s success, including:  
- The significance of teamwork and open communication  
- Balancing creativity with organizational discipline  
- Promoting active participation across all technical tracks  
- Maintaining continuity and documentation across project cycles  
- Building a sustainable and inclusive club culture  

The ex-core members emphasized the importance of leadership qualities such as empathy, clarity, and accountability in managing club responsibilities effectively.

Towards the conclusion of the session, an **interactive brainstorming exercise** was conducted.  
Each current core member was presented with **realistic club management scenarios** and asked to propose **strategies and solutions**.  

The activity aimed to:  
- Enhance critical and strategic thinking  
- Encourage collaborative problem-solving  
- Prepare members for decision-making in real-world situations related to club functioning  

This exercise provided valuable insights into how the current core could handle challenges while fostering teamwork and innovation.
