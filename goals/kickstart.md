Duration: 18th September – 8th November 2025
Phase: Kickstart Phase

## Introduction

The Kickstart Phase of DevTrack (Cycle 2025) symbolized the official relaunch of the club’s activities for the new academic cycle. This phase was centered on rebuilding momentum, strengthening participation, and re-establishing DevTrack’s creative and technical presence among the students of the Computer Science, IT, and Computer departments.

The primary goals of this phase were to plan the upcoming cycle efficiently, **recruit enthusiastic new members**, establish a strong mentorship structure, and design engaging activities that would set the tone for a vibrant year ahead. With meticulous planning, coordination, and enthusiasm, this phase successfully laid the foundation for a productive and collaborative cycle.

---

## Planning Phase (18th – 22nd September 2025)

The Kickstart Phase began with an intensive week of planning and preparation. During this period, the core team focused on **designing creative posters, obtaining necessary permissions, and organizing the overall schedule for recruitment and orientation**.

The team held multiple brainstorming sessions to define the objectives of the new cycle and ensure that each activity aligned with DevTrack’s vision of fostering innovation and technical excellence. This phase also marked the beginning of collaborations between mentors and the core team to finalize strategies for outreach, recruitment, and event execution.

---

## Public Pitch and Introduction (30th September 2025)

On 30th September 2025, DevTrack made its official comeback through a unique and captivating **public pitch** delivered across the CSE, IT, and Computer departments.
The core team visited all first-year and second-year classrooms to introduce the club, share its vision, and inspire students to become part of the DevTrack community.

The day’s highlight was the team’s creative and energetic presentation style, which left a lasting impression on students and faculty alike. Simultaneously, the poster-making and display process was initiated across the campus, marking the start of the recruitment campaign.

---

## Mentor Selection and Member Registration (1st October 2025)

Following the pitch, the team proceeded with the **selection of mentors** from various technical domains. Mentors were chosen based on their expertise in fields such as Artificial Intelligence, Web Development, Cybersecurity, and Programming. Their role was to guide and support the new members through various club initiatives.

Member registration officially opened on 1st October 2025, and the response was overwhelming. Within a short span, the club received around 35 registrations from highly motivated students eager to explore technology, creativity, and innovation under the DevTrack banner.

---

## Recruitment Drive (5th – 6th October 2025)

The online recruitment process began on 5th October 2025 and continued the next day. The process was divided into three interactive slots, ensuring that every participant had ample opportunity to express their creativity, logical reasoning, and problem-solving abilities.

The recruitment was designed to evaluate not just technical skills but also enthusiasm, collaboration, and commitment — qualities that define a true DevTrack member. The sessions witnessed lively participation, constructive discussions, and remarkable enthusiasm from students.

---

## Announcement of Selected Members (11th October 2025)

After careful evaluation and discussions among the mentors and core team, the final list of selected candidates was compiled. On **11th October 2025**, the official selection mails were sent to the new members, marking the successful conclusion of the recruitment process.

This milestone represented the transition into a new era for DevTrack — one that brought together a blend of experienced mentors, a dedicated core team, and newly recruited members eager to contribute to the club’s growth.

---

## Orientation Planning and Preparation (October – Early November 2025)

Once recruitment concluded, the team shifted focus towards planning the Orientation Program, scheduled for 6th November 2025.
The preparation phase involved designing promotional posters, preparing invitation cards, and finalizing interactive activities to make the event both welcoming and engaging.

Games such as **Crossword and Bingo** were selected to bring an element of fun and connection among participants.
Invitations were sent to seniors, super seniors, and ex-core members of DevTrack to celebrate the spirit of continuity and mentorship that the club upholds.

The entire orientation plan was designed to strike a perfect balance between formal introduction and informal bonding, ensuring that new members felt connected and inspired from the very beginning.

---

## Engagement and Activity Strategy

To maintain long-term engagement and ensure consistent member interaction, the team introduced a creative initiative called **“Question of the Day”** Challenge.
This activity aimed to keep the community active through daily technical or fun questions, encouraging members to think critically and participate actively in discussions.

To further boost participation, the team decided to recognize the most active member of each month with exciting rewards such as a **1-month premium subscription** to popular platforms like **YouTube, Netflix, or Spotify**.
This reward-based system not only motivated members but also fostered a healthy and enthusiastic learning environment within the club.

---

## Outcomes and Impact

The Kickstart Phase was a resounding success, achieving all its planned objectives on time. The recruitment drive resulted in a strong and diverse group of members ready to contribute to the club’s projects. Mentors were successfully assigned, and clear communication channels were established for smooth collaboration.

The phase also helped re-establish DevTrack’s presence within the department, generating curiosity and excitement among students.
Through thoughtful planning, the core team ensured that each activity reinforced DevTrack’s mission — to inspire, innovate, and collaborate.

This phase significantly strengthened the internal structure of the club and prepared it for the upcoming Orientation and Project phases.

---

## Conclusion

The Kickstart Phase (September – November 2025) served as a cornerstone for DevTrack’s revival and growth. It demonstrated the power of teamwork, foresight, and creativity in building a thriving technical community.

From initial planning to recruitment, mentor selection, and orientation preparation, every milestone reflected the collective spirit of the DevTrack team.
The enthusiasm shown by the members and mentors reaffirmed DevTrack’s commitment to excellence, collaboration, and innovation.

As the phase concluded, the foundation was firmly set for the next stages — the Orientation Phase and Active Project Phase, where ideas will transform into impactful initiatives, continuing the legacy of DevTrack’s innovation-driven journey.
